Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Steps:

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

2. Heat a pan and pour the batter.

3. Cook until golden brown on both sides.

Omelette

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Eggs 2
Salt To taste
Butter 1 tbsp

Steps:

1. Beat the eggs with salt.

2. Melt butter in a pan.

3. Cook eggs until set.
